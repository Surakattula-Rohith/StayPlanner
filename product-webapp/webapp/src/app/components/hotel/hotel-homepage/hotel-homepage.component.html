Steps to List Your Hotel:

  1. Sign Up: Create your hotel owner account on StayPlanner or log in if you already have one.
  2. Property Details: Provide essential information about your property, including its name, location, type (e.g., hotel, bed and breakfast), and a brief description that highlights its unique features.
  3. Amenities and Services: Showcase what sets your property apart. Does it have a pool, spa, restaurant, or conference facilities? Let travelers know what they can look forward to.
  4. Upload Photos: Add high-quality images that capture the essence of your property. Highlight the rooms, common areas, views, and any other features that guests will love.
  5. Availability and Rates: Set up your property's availability calendar and rates. You have full control over pricing and can offer special deals for different seasons.
  6. Direct Communication: Guests can inquire about bookings directly through our platform. You'll receive notifications and can communicate with potential guests to ensure a seamless experience.
